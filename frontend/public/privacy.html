User Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to delete your data from Weary, please follow these steps:

  1. Click the "Logout" button in the top navigation bar. This clears all local session data.
  2. To revoke app permissions from your social account:
    • Facebook: Go to Settings & Privacy > Settings > Apps and Websites. Find "Weary" and click Remove.
    • Google: Go to your Google Account > Security > Third-party apps with account access. Find "Weary" and click Remove Access.
